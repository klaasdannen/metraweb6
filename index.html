Making Things Communicate

metraTec is a specialist for wireless electronics in the fields Identification (RFID), Realtime Localization (IPS/RTLS) and connectivity in the "Internet of Things" (IoT). With our products and services we help our customers world wide track their supply chains in real time, identify persons and objects and localize them in a building, connect devices to each other and the cloud as well as many more applications.

If you have an idea for an innovative use of RF and communication technology and you're looking for a qualified and flexible partner, please contact us!

Radiofrequency Identification (RFID)

Identification of people and objects without line of sight and without batteries

Indoor Localization

Locate people and objects inside buildings for more efficient and secure company processes

Internet of Things

Components and solutions for data communication of industrial devices for the "Internet of Things" - via cable and wirelessly.

OEM Design

You've got a project where you need support with electronics design? We can help you with that!
