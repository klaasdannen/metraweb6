Need Support?

If you have questions or trouble with our products,

Products for IoT, RFID and Localization are not always easy to use. Some of the products require special knowledge about the physics of radio waves. Other systems have to be integrated into more or less complicated IT systems. We design our products to be as easy to use as possible. Still, sometimes questions remain which hopefully can be answered in this part of the website. In this section you find:

  • Frequently Asked Questions about our products and technology in general
  • Download links to all our datasheets, documentation and other ressources
