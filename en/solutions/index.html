Solutions

Need inspiration for a good IoT use case?
Have a look at our innovative industry IoT solutions

"Internet of Things" is a buzz word used for a variety of applications in almost any industry. For us at metraTec "IoT" means any solution that connects real world objects to IT systems - often using RF technologies like:

  • RFID with cheap, passive (batteryless) transponders
  • Indoor tracking and RTLS
  • Other cell network technology (GSM or LTE)
